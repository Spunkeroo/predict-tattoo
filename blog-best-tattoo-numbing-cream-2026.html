Best Tattoo Numbing Creams 2026: Top 5 Reviewed

Numbing cream can make the difference between a 2-hour tattoo session feeling manageable and feeling like torture. Chicago's top tattoo artists generally accept numbing cream use — just ask your artist before your appointment. Here are the most effective options tested by real Chicago tattoo clients.

Best Overall: Zensa Numbing Cream 5% Lidocaine ($45)

The most recommended numbing cream by tattoo artists nationwide. 5% lidocaine (the maximum available OTC) provides 2-3 hours of effective numbing. Vitamin E base moisturizes skin for better ink absorption. Apply 45-60 minutes before your session, cover with plastic wrap. Does not affect tattoo quality or healing. Used and recommended by Chicago shops including Deluxe Tattoo, Great Lakes Tattoo, and Speakeasy Custom Tattoo.

Best Value: Uber Numb 5% Lidocaine ($20)

Same 5% lidocaine concentration as Zensa at less than half the price. Slightly thicker consistency — apply a generous layer 60 minutes before your appointment. Water-based formula washes off cleanly. Some artists report it makes skin slightly more slippery during application — communicate with your artist. Available on Amazon with next-day delivery to Chicago.

How to Apply Numbing Cream

  1. Clean the tattoo area with soap and water 75 minutes before your appointment
  2. Apply a thick layer (about 1mm thick) over the entire area to be tattooed
  3. Cover tightly with plastic wrap — Saran Wrap works perfectly
  4. Leave on for 45-60 minutes. Do NOT exceed 90 minutes
  5. Remove plastic wrap and wipe cream off just before your artist begins
  6. Some artists will reapply during breaks for long sessions — bring extra cream
