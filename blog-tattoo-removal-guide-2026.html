Tattoo Removal Guide 2026: Costs, Methods & What to Expect

Tattoo removal technology has improved dramatically. PicoSure and PicoWay lasers can now remove tattoos in 4-8 sessions that previously required 10-15+. Chicago has some of the best removal clinics in the Midwest. Here's what to expect.

How Laser Removal Works

Q-switched and picosecond lasers fire ultra-short pulses of light that shatter ink particles into microscopic fragments. Your immune system's white blood cells then carry these fragments away through the lymphatic system. Each session removes a layer of ink — multiple sessions are needed because the laser can only reach so deep per treatment.

Factors That Affect Removal

  • Ink color: Black is easiest to remove. Green and blue are harder. Yellow, white, and fluorescent are the hardest
  • Ink depth: Professional tattoos use deeper, denser ink than amateur tattoos — takes more sessions
  • Skin tone: Lighter skin responds faster to treatment. Darker skin requires more careful laser settings to avoid pigment changes
  • Location: Tattoos closer to the heart (chest, upper arms) fade faster due to better circulation. Feet and hands are slowest
  • Age of tattoo: Older tattoos (10+ years) are easier to remove — the ink has already begun fading naturally

  • Removery — River North. National chain with Chicago's most advanced PicoWay laser. Free consultations. Payment plans available. The highest-volume removal clinic in the Midwest
  • Invisible Ink Tattoo Removers — Multiple locations. PicoSure technology. Competitive pricing, often $50-$100 less per session than competitors

What to Expect

Pain: Similar to getting the tattoo — rubber band snap with heat. Most clinics apply topical lidocaine 30 minutes before treatment. Cooling devices during treatment reduce discomfort significantly. Sessions last 5-30 minutes depending on tattoo size.

Healing: Expect redness, swelling, and blistering for 1-2 weeks. Keep the area clean and covered. Avoid sun exposure on the treated area. Wait 6-8 weeks between sessions to allow full healing and maximum ink clearance.
